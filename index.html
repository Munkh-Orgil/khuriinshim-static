Short Description of Our Start-Up

Khuryn Shim, is developing Mongolian organic garlic honey and we do workshops on how to grow garlic.Our business cluster is based to tackle the problem of consumers are being unable to use organic products in their food vice versa local independent farmers face hardship making profit of their harvests. Khuryn Shim cluster is promoting the number of local farmers in the Mongolian garlic market and opening new income opportunities to the locals.


Our aim is to bring private and public support to the table to sustain Mongolian farmers’ markets.

Can you use garlic with milk?
Yes, you can. In order to make it more tastier, you can drink it with hot milk. It is a great remedy for curing flu.
What are the benefits of garlic?
There are many benefits. It has been used by humans for thousands of years and was used in Ancient Egypt for both culinary purposes and its health and therapeutic benefits. Dillydally sulfide, a compound in garlic, was 100 times more effective than two popular antibiotics in fighting the Campylobacter bacterium, according to a study published in the Journal of Antimicrobial Chemotherapy. It can Combat sickness, including the common Cold. Garlic is also very beneficial to your immunity. Answer source
